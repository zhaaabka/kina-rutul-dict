éwčʼur

Orthography: евчӀур
Part of speech: verb
Glossing label: INSIDE-move

Meanings

  1. enter — входить
    • rɨš χala erčʼura / рыш хала ерчӀура — ‘Девочка зашла в дом’

Inflection

Perfective, Gender 3 éwčʼur
Imperfective, Gender 3 óˤwʁura ~ áˤbʁura ~ ébčʼura
Infinitive, Gender 3 éwčʼus
Imperative, Gender 3 ewíčʼ
Prohibitive, Gender 3 amóˤwuʁ ~ aˤmábʁ
