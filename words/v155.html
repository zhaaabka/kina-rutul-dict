seqípʼir

verb

Orthography: сехъипӀир
Part of speech: verb
Glossing label: DOWN-RE-pull

Collected by NB

Meanings

  1. ‘разрушать’ — ‘destroy’
    • insanaše mas seqitʼir
      инсанаше мас сехъитӀир
      ‘Бригада разрушила стену’
  2. ‘снять’ — ‘take off (cloths)’
    • zad χalijila ǯuna seqipʼir
      зад халийила джуна сехъипӀир
      ‘Я снял повязку’

Inflection

Perfective, Gender 3 seqípʼir
Imperfective, Gender 3 seqwílera
Infinitive, Gender 3 seqípʼes
Imperative, Gender 3 seqípʼe
Prohibitive, Gender 3 seqméwliʔ
